FAQs

Who are we ?
Look for worker is an initiative of the Federation of Artisans. The Fédération des Artisans brings together craft businesses legally established in Luxembourg. Luxembourg's craft industry has 7,000 companies with some 90,000 employees.
How does my quote request work?
Requesting a quote is simple, quick and without obligation. I fill out the information related to my request and my contact information. My request is then sent to companies in my region who contact me to offer me their offers. All I have to do is compare and choose the offer I prefer!
Why use the Wedo service?
Wedo allows me to select the best Luxembourg craftsmen to carry out my work and to compare the offers that correspond to my requests. In addition to being free, the Wedo service is simple and fast!
Am I committed by my quote request?
No, my quote request does not bind me in any way. Once the various quotes from the craftsmen have been received, it is up to me to follow up or not.
Which companies will respond to me?
All companies present on the Wedo portal are artisans based in Luxembourg and are subject to accreditations and operating authorizations issued by the Luxembourg authorities.
